Demo 2: Accordion Navigation in Side Panel

 

Click the menu button at the top left corner of the screen to view the side navigation panel.

The accordion menu can be easily converted to side navigation panel if you wrap the menu with a Super Panel.

You can configure the Super Panel to slide out or fade out the menu from any positions in any directions.
