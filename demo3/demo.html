Demo 3: Make the Side Menu Responsive

 

Resize your window to see how the right navigation bar is hiddened, and how it is converted to a side menu that you can expand with a button.

With the Super Panel tool, the side menu bar on the right will become a menu icon when the screen is smaller than a preset breakpoint.

This is a simple responsive side menu solution.
